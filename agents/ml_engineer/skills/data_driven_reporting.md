# Data-Driven Reporting

Purpose: Design analytical reports that combine quantitative evidence with structural readability.

## Core Rules

1. Executive Summary First
   - Start every report with an executive summary containing key findings and high-level recommendations.

2. Structure with Evidence
   - Every claim in the body of the report must be immediately backed by a referenced dataset table, statistical metric, or visualization.

3. Declare Limitations
   - Dedicate a section to data constraints, assumptions, and potential sources of bias or correlation vs. causation limits.
